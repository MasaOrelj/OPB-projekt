Master the art of spellcasting and enchantments in the Charms class. Learn to manipulate objects, create illusions, and charm your way through various situations.

Transfiguration

Unleash your ability to transform and alter the form of objects and creatures with the Transfiguration class. Turn teacups into mice and learn advanced transformation spells.

Explore the mysteries of the night sky and celestial bodies in the Astronomy class. Learn about the stars, planets, and their influence on magic and life at Hogwarts.

Develop your inner sight and learn to foresee the future in the Divination class. Explore various methods like tea leaf reading, crystal ball gazing, and more.

Delve into the world of non-magical beings and understand their culture, technology, and way of life in the Muggle Studies class. Bridge the gap between the wizarding and muggle worlds.

Study of Ancient Runes

Unravel the ancient symbols, languages, and texts of wizards past in the Study of Ancient Runes class. Decipher powerful enchantments and forgotten spells.

Learn the art of disappearing and reappearing at will with the Apparition class. Master the technique of magical teleportation to navigate swiftly and secretly.

Unlock the wisdom of the Tarot cards and gain insights into the past, present, and future. Discover hidden truths and explore the realms of divination.

Library Science

Unearth the rich tapestry of magical history in the History of Magic class. Explore the legends, events, and key figures that shaped the wizarding world as we know it.
